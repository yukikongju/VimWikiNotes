Zettelkasten Note Taking Methods

The Zettelkasten Note Taking Methods State that we should keep track of 3 types of notes:

  1. Fleeting Notes: Spontaneous Notes
  2. Literature Notes: Concepts from Reading
  3. Permanent Notes: Concept that seems to stick when we review our fleeting and litterature notes

Presently, I like to keep my fleeting notes in my pink notebook, but I would like to have to create a notetaking systems for everything I read.
